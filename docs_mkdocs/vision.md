# Vision

`PADO Hologram` is intended to be more than a code drop.

We want it to become a shared, maintained home for holography and computational
imaging research, where people from different backgrounds can build together,
learn from one another, and inspire one another.

That includes contributors coming from:

- computer science
- electrical engineering
- optics and photonics
- physics
- psychology and perception science
- neighboring disciplines that care about holography

We also want to move beyond fragmented one-off attempts and disconnected tooling.
The aim is to grow a reusable, testable, documented stack that makes collaboration easier.

Older frameworks such as [`holotorch`](https://github.com/facebookresearch/holotorch)
helped show why higher-level CGH workflow layers matter. `PADO Hologram` is not
trying to clone them, but to carry that lesson forward in a smaller, cleaner,
and more maintainable PADO-native form.
