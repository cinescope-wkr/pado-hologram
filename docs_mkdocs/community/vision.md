# Vision

`PADO Hologram` is intended to be more than a code release.

The long-term goal is to build a shared, maintained home for holography and
computational imaging research that people can actually reuse, contribute to,
and grow over time.

## Why This Matters

Many research codebases in this area are valuable but fragmented. They often:

- focus on a single result
- remain lightly maintained
- are difficult to extend
- are hard for newcomers to understand

This repository is trying to move in the opposite direction.

## Community Scope

We want the project to be welcoming to people from:

- computer science
- electrical engineering
- optics and photonics
- physics
- perception science
- neighboring disciplines that care about holography

The shared motivation is more important than a specific academic label.

## Historical Context

Projects such as [`holotorch`](https://github.com/facebookresearch/holotorch)
helped make it clear that higher-level holography workflow layers matter.
`PADO Hologram` is not trying to reproduce them exactly, but to carry those
lessons forward in a smaller and more maintainable PADO-native form.
